Instructions for taking the EUM-O (EUM Organization)

You have to rank a set of 20 adjectives three times: once to describe how you see your organisation today, then to describe how you would like to see your organisation ideally, and finally to describe how you see most other organisations around you. You can do the ranking within each of the three columns by dragging the adjectives from the list on the left to the numbered rows on the right, and can rearrange the order if required by moving the adjectives up and down in the table on the right.

1. For the column titled “ORGANISATION AS-IS”, drag the adjectives from the left column and place them in the right column in the order that you believe they apply to your organisation currently Rank 1 best describes how you see the organisation today, while rank 20 applies to your organisation the least.

2. For the column titled “ORGANISATION WISHED FOR”, drag the adjectives from the left column and place them in the right column in the order that you would *like* them to apply to your organisation. Rank 1 describes what you would most like to see in your ideal organisation, while rank 20 shows what you would like to see the least.

3. In the column titled “MOST OTHER ORGANIZATIONS”, drag the adjectives from the left column and place them in the right column to describe how you see most other organisations. Rank 1 describes the top feature you see in most other organisations, while rank 20 indicates what you see in them the least. [While all other organisations are of course not the same, we do typically hold some generalisations about how other organisations are – we invite you use that perspective to rank the adjectives on this screen.]

4. At any time in this ranking process, you can use the left and right arrowheads next the ranking columns to move back and forth between the three lists described above.

Once you have completed ranking all the 20 adjectives in each of the three lists, please click on the “SUBMIT” button. This will bring up a window that will show you your rankings across the three lists. If you don’t wish to make any changes, please click on the “Confirm Submit” button.

If you feel you want to change any of the rankings, click “Cancel” at the bottom of the screen, which will take you back to the ranking screens. You can then click on “Submit” again, review your rankings, and then click “Confirm Submit”.
