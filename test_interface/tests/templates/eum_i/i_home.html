Instructions for taking the EUM-I

You have to rank a set of 15 adjectives three times: once to describe how you see yourself today, then to describe how you would like to see yourself ideally, and finally to describe how you see most other people in the world around you. You can do the ranking within each of the three columns by dragging the adjectives from the list on the left to the numbered rows on the right, and can rearrange the order if required by moving the adjectives up and down in the table on the right.

1. For the column titled “I AM…”, drag the adjectives from the left column and place them in the right column in the order that you believe they apply to you currently. Rank 1 describes you the best, and rank 15 applies to you the least.

2. For the column titled “I WOULD LIKE TO BE…”, drag the adjectives from the left column and place them in the right column in the order that you would like them to apply to you – in other words, how you would ideally want to be. Rank 1 is what you most want to see in yourself, while rank 15 is what you would least want to see in yourself.

3. For the column titled “OTHER PEOPLE ARE…”, drag the adjectives from the left column and place them in the right column to reflect how you see most other people. Rank 1 describes your view of other people the best, like rank 15 describes what you see the least in other people. [While all other people are of course not the same, we do typically hold some views on how the world is – we invite you use that perspective to rank the adjectives on this screen.]

Once you have completed ranking all the 15 adjectives in each of the three lists, please click on the “SUBMIT” button. This will bring up a window that will show you your rankings across the three lists. If you don’t wish to make any changes, please click on the “Submit” button.

If you feel you want to change any of the rankings, click “Cancel” at the bottom of the screen, which will take you back to the ranking screens. You can then click on “Submit” again, review your rankings, and then click “Confirm Submit”.
